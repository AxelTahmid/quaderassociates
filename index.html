For Job Seekers

How to Get a Job Abroad

We make the process simple. Come to our office and we take care of the rest.

01

Visit Our Office

Come to our Banani, Dhaka office. Bring your NID card, passport (or passport application receipt), and any trade or skill certificates you have.

02

Free Registration

Register your skills and preferred destination for free. Our staff will explain all options available to you in your language.

03

Job Matching

We match your profile to verified job orders from licensed overseas employers and contact you when a suitable position is available.

04

Visa & Documentation

We process your visa, BMET clearance, medical fitness test, and pre-departure orientation — all handled by our team.

05

Fly & Earn

We provide airport assistance on your departure day. Our office stays available to your family back home.
